Heroes of the pandemic

  • 1
  • 2

2020, at that time I had just started my internship at Blackbird Studio and working from home was not as present as now, without much problem I could integrate this development as an activity in my internship with Blackbird.

The idea was to create something simple and direct to publish with my fellow teamates, from this arose to make a "runner" where you dodge them by sliding the finger by screen and pick up some powerups. To earn points you have to collect EPIS (protective equipment) that are spread over the level.

Once implemented the designs of the different patterns of streets that were instantiated randomly only remained to add the shader of curvature and make a selection character menu.

In a week and a half it was finished, unfortunately, at that time the apple store and the play store did not allow the publication of nothing related to the Covid, to this day I still do not know why, even so you can play it for free on the page of the Camara de Comercio.
