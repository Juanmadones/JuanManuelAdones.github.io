OOZZ

  • 1
  • 2

OOZZ is an online meeting application made in Unity where you manage a custom avatar, you can access through your own account and once joined to a server you can talk, chat, make presentations, share your computer screen or webcam and interact with other users in various ways.

This project was done while I was working at Save Games Studio, in which:

I create a user login and an edit menu of your personal data, a global text chat per server using Photon, a list of connected users by which you could invite to a private text chat and a private voice chat to the selected user. Depending on your role you could ban or mute users also in this menu, I create personalized panels for each user that were activated when you clicked on them containing information about that user and links to their social networks, implement the possibility of sharing the screen of your computer to all connected users and in case of having webcam connected you could also share your image in real time, also implement interactive elements such as chairs, blackboards, screens, animations, images...

The application contains 2 different scenarios where you can have meetings and make events.
